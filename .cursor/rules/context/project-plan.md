**II. The Client Journey: From Landing to Launch**

This is the step-by-step flow a potential client will experience on your website.

**Step 1: The Landing (Hero Section)**
The client arrives. The first impression must be immediate, clear, and compelling. They instantly understand what you do and why it's different.

**Step 2: The "Smart" Intake Form**
The client decides to engage. They scroll down to a multi-part form that feels less like a form and more like a guided consultation.

*   **Part A: The Project Brief**
    *   **Business Name & Industry:** To personalize the AI's output.
    *   **Primary Goal:** Dropdown (Leads, Sales, Portfolio, etc.).
    *   **Project Description:** A text area where they describe their vision.
    *   **Key Features:** Checkboxes (Contact Form, Blog, E-commerce, etc.).

*   **Part B: The Domain Selector (The "Wow" Moment)**
    *   **UI:** A clean input field with the prompt: "What's your dream domain name?"
    *   **Functionality:**
        1.  The user types `mycoolbusiness.com` and clicks "Check Availability."
        2.  Your site hits a Next.js API route that calls the **Vercel Domains API** to check for availability.
        3.  **If Available:** A green checkmark appears with the message: "Great news! `mycoolbusiness.com` is available and will be included with your plan."
        4.  **If Unavailable:** A red 'X' appears: "Unfortunately, that domain is taken." Immediately following, a loading state appears: "Our AI is finding some great alternatives for you..."
        5.  Your backend sends the business name, industry, and the desired domain to an AI model.
        6.  The AI returns 5-7 similar, available alternatives (e.g., `getmycoolbusiness.com`, `mycoolbusiness.io`).
        7.  These options are displayed beautifully for the user to choose from. The client selects one, and it's saved with their form data.

**Step 3: The AI-Powered Offerings Page**
Upon submitting the form, the client is immediately taken to a personalized results page. This is the payoff.

*   **Headline:** "Here are 3 custom-tailored options for [Client's Business Name]"
*   **The Tiers:** A three-column layout displays the "Launchpad," "Growth Engine," and "Business OS" tiers. The description and feature list for each tier have been **dynamically generated by your AI** based on the client's form input, making it feel incredibly bespoke.

**Step 4: The Checkout**
Each tier has a "Choose Plan" button. Clicking this takes them to a Stripe Checkout page to pay the one-time setup fee and initiate the monthly subscription.

**Step 5: The Confirmation**
Upon successful payment, the client receives a beautifully designed confirmation email with their project details, next steps, and timeline. You also receive a notification email with the client's project brief to begin work.

**V. The Backend AI Logic**

*   **AI Project Brief Generator:**
    *   **Trigger:** User submits the project form.
    *   **Action:** An API route sends the form data (business name, industry, description, features) to a GPT-4o model.
    *   **Prompt:** "You are an expert web consultant. Analyze the following client brief and generate personalized descriptions and feature lists for our 'Launchpad', 'Growth Engine', and 'Business OS' tiers. Ensure the features requested by the user are placed in the appropriate tier. Output must be in clean JSON."
*   **AI Domain Suggester:**
    *   **Trigger:** User's desired domain is unavailable.
    *   **Action:** An API route sends the business name, industry, and desired domain to a GPT-4o model.
    *   **Prompt:** "You are a branding expert. A client in the '{industry}' industry wants the domain '{domain_name}', but it's taken. Generate 7 creative, professional, and available alternative domain names. Prioritize .com, but also suggest relevant .io, .co, or .app options. Output as a JSON array."
    *   **Verification:** The results from the AI would then be run through the Vercel API's bulk availability check to ensure they are indeed available before showing them to the user.

**VI. Email Automation with Resend**

All transactional emails are powered by **Resend**, ensuring reliable delivery with beautiful, branded templates.

*   **Client Welcome Email:**
    *   **Trigger:** Successful Stripe payment webhook received.
    *   **Action:** A Stripe webhook handler processes the successful payment and triggers a Resend email.
    *   **Recipients:** The client's email address (captured during checkout).
    *   **Email Content:**
        *   **Subject:** "Welcome to [Your Agency Name]! Your [Tier Name] Build Starts Now 🚀"
        *   **Body Includes:**
            *   Personal welcome message
            *   Order summary (tier selected, domain name, setup fee, monthly subscription)
            *   Project timeline and expected delivery date
            *   What happens next (kickoff process)
            *   Access to a client portal/dashboard (if applicable)
            *   Your contact information for any questions
    *   **Design:** React Email components for beautiful, responsive HTML emails that work across all email clients.

*   **Agency Notification Email:**
    *   **Trigger:** Same Stripe webhook event as client email.
    *   **Recipients:** Your agency email address.
    *   **Email Content:**
        *   **Subject:** "🎉 New Client Signup: [Business Name] - [Tier Name]"
        *   **Body Includes:**
            *   Complete project brief (all form data)
            *   Selected tier and pricing
            *   Domain name chosen
            *   Client contact information
            *   Link to Stripe dashboard for payment details
            *   Formatted for quick review and project kickoff
    *   **Purpose:** Immediately alerts you to begin work with all necessary client information.

*   **Additional Email Triggers (Optional/Future):**
    *   **Project Milestone Updates:** "Your site is 50% complete!"
    *   **Launch Notification:** "Your website is live!"
    *   **Monthly Subscription Receipt:** Automated from Stripe, but can be customized via Resend.
    *   **AI Editor Onboarding:** Tutorial email sent after first login.
    *   **Payment Failed:** Reminder emails for failed subscription payments.

*   **Technical Implementation:**
    *   **Setup:** Resend API key stored in environment variables.
    *   **Email Templates:** Built with `@react-email/components` for maintainable, reusable templates.
    *   **Webhook Security:** Stripe webhook signatures verified before processing.
    *   **Error Handling:** Failed email sends are logged and retried with exponential backoff.
    *   **Testing:** Use Resend's test mode during development to preview emails without sending.
