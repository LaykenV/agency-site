5-star animation implementation notes

- Inline SVG stars to minimize JS; reserve width/height to prevent layout shift.

- Stagger fill: 150–200ms per star; 100ms initial delay; 1.05 scale on fill; ease-out; total ~1–1.5s.

- When star 5 fills, fade in qualifier microcopy and then reveal headline/subhead (or preload headline and only fade the microcopy to minimize motion).

- prefers-reduced-motion: render fully filled stars with no animation.
