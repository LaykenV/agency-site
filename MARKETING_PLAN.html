5Scripts library

Memorize the flow, not the words. The goal is to sound like a neighbor, not a salesman.

Drop-in (90 seconds, owner present)

"Hey [Name], I'm Layken with Acadiana Web Design — local guy, live right here in Youngsville. Veteran. I won't take your time. I ran a quick performance check on your site, thought you might want to see it. No pitch, just leaving it for you. My card's in there if anything stands out. Have a good one."Hand over packet. Eye contact. Walk out. Do NOT pitch. Do NOT linger. The audit is the pitch. The follow-up call is the close.

Drop-in (owner NOT present)

"No problem — could you make sure [Name] gets this? It's a site report I put together for them. Thanks."Hand to whoever's at the counter. Leave. Don't try to extract info or schedule.

Drop-in follow-up call (3–5 days after, Wed or Thu)

"Hey [Name], it's Layken — dropped off the site report at your shop Monday. Just calling to see if you had a chance to look at it. [Pause. Let them answer.] If YES: Cool. Any questions on what I flagged? If NO: No worries. The biggest thing I saw was [their #1 issue from the audit]. Want me to send you a quick walkthrough by email, or want to grab 15 min next week to look at it together?"Goal: book the 15-min Cal.com call. NOT to close. Close happens on that call.

Sign-spot warm call (Tuesday evening)

"Hey, is this [Business Name]? — Hi [Name if you got it], my name's Layken, I run Acadiana Web Design here in Youngsville. I saw your [truck on Chemin Metairie / yard sign on Verot School / etc.] earlier today and went to check out your website — looks like y'all don't have one yet, is that right? [Let them answer.]"These are WARM because: you saw their truck/sign, you know their service area, you confirmed the gap. Not cold dialing. 7What NOT to do this week

Your instinct will pull you toward these. The 90-day plan is explicit: they're either deferred to Tier 2 (Day 30+) or skipped entirely. Re-read this list every morning before opening anything new.

  • No mass cold dialing. Sign-spotted calls only — you've seen them, gap is confirmed, that's warm. A month of paralysis on cold dialing is the reason this plan exists.
  • No Google Ads yet. Day 30+. You don't have conversion data to optimize against.
  • No Facebook ads / retargeting yet. Day 30+.
  • No new tooling. The pipeline + a Google Sheet are enough. No CRM, no email sequencer, no AI marketing agent service.
  • No more than 3 marketing searches this week. You can't action what you generate.
  • No drop-ins outside Youngsville (Broussard overflow Thu only if pool is thin). 45-min drives for one stop = wasted hour.
  • No same-day rerun after a rough drop-in. Move on. Activity beats perfection.
  • No LinkedIn content / TikTok / IG posting. Wrong audience, content treadmill, won't move the needle this week.
  • No outsourced marketing services like Enrichlabs. Your pipeline already does this — outsourcing the moat kills the moat.
  • No pitching during a drop-in. Hand over the packet, walk out. The follow-up call is the pitch.
  • No third nudge on review-ask non-responders. One reminder, then drop it for 3 weeks.
